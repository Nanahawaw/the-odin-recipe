Plantain Fritata

A Unique Culinary Delight!

Description
Plantain Pizza, often simply referred to as 'Plantain', is a delectable and nutritious dish that brilliantly marries the rich, sweet flavors of ripe plantain with the savory notes of egg. While its preparation may remind many of the beloved pizza, this dish stands out with its unique blend of ingredients, offering a wholesome alternative to traditional pizza varieties. The layering of the ripe plantain base with toppings evokes the familiar pizza aesthetic, but with an unmistakable tropical twist. Often hailed for its health benefits, this innovative dish not only tantalizes the taste buds but also nourishes the body, making it a favorite choice for those seeking both flavor and nutrition.

ingredients

  • 3 ripe plantains
  • 2 table spoons vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 medium red bell pepper, cut in strips
  • 1 medium green bell pepper, cut in strips
  • 11 medium yellow bell pepper, cut in strips
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon tumeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon thyme or any herb/li>
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground pepper
  • 1 seasoning cube
  • 1 cup chopped sausages
  • 6-7 large eggs
  • Vegetable oil for frying plantain
  • Salt to taste

Steps/Preparation Method

  1. Cut the ripe plantain in rings, fry and set aside
  2. Heat up 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil in a pan
  3. Sauté the garlic, onions, sausages and peppers
  4. In a bowl, break in the eggs and whisk
  5. Spice the eggs with the ground pepper, seasoning cubes, tumeric, thyme and salt to taste
  6. Pour in the sautéed peppers mixture into the eggs and mix.
  7. Bake on low heat for about 30 minutes or until the egg is firm.
  8. If using a frying pan, do same and cover with foil and place over low heat until cooked.
